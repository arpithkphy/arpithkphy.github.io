Arpith Kumar

THEORETICAL PHYSICIST

I am a theoretical physicist. My research interest revolves around non-perturbative simulations of field-theoretic systems. Numerical studies of quantum field theories, quantum many-body systems, and condensed matter physics are of particular interest to me.

I am also enthusiastic about working on challenging problems at the forefront of physics, including but not restricted to molecular mechanics, non-equilibrium dynamics, and biophysics.
